RAPPER Fabolous rose to prominence for his popular hits from the last two decades.

After decades of success, fans are curious to know more about Fabolous and his number of children.

Who are Fabolous' children?

On February 16, 2008, Fabolous and his then-girlfriend Emily Bustamente welcomed their first child together.

Although their son Johan is Fabolous' first kid, the rap music star was already a stepfather to Emily's first daughter Taina Williams.

On June 2, 2015, the pair revealed that Emily gave birth to her and Fabolous' second son Jonas.

Regarding his life as a father, the New York-born musician told MTV: "It's hard to put in words, but it's a blessing.

On October 10, 2020, the two became parents again with the arrival of their daughter Journey.

Who is Fabolous' stepdaughter Taina Williams?

Prior to his three children with Love & Hip Hop alum Emily B, the Make Me Better hitmaker was a stepdaughter to his girlfriend's first daughter Taina.

Born on April 1, 1998, Taina garnered her own following as a social media influencer.

In January 2019, she and Chicago-born rapper G Herbo announced they were officially dating.

On May 27, 2021, Taina gave birth to her first child with Herbo.

Though their son Essex is the former's first baby, Herbo is already a father to his son Yosohn with Instagram influencer Ari Fletcher.

On May 20, 2022, Taina became a mother for the second time when she gave birth to her and Herbo's daughter, Emmy.
