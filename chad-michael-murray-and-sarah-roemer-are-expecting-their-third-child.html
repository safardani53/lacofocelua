Chad Michael Murray And Sarah Roemer Are Expecting Their Third Child

February 19, 2024
blog

Chad Michael Murray and his wife Sarah Roemer are expecting their third child. The pair began dating in 2014 and announced their engagement the following year. The couple has two additional children, including a boy born in 2015 and a daughter born in 2017. Chad posted a photo of Sarah showing off her baby belly on Instagram, with the remark, “Our third baby is on the way.” In addition, he wrote:

“Ps- please excuse the poor quality of the photo- I’m still on IPhone 8 because I love the button and won’t give it up.” I get it when I see someone looking at my phone with button envy.”

Chad then shared the same photo on his Instagram story with the caption:

“We’re expecting our third child.” We’re going to need a larger vehicle. “It’s minivan time.”

Chad Michael Murray and Sarah Roemer’s children

Chad Michael Murray and Sarah Roemer originally met in 2014 while working on the Crackle TV action-thriller series Chosen. He married actress and model Sarah Roemer in 2015 after a short courtship. His agent disclosed the same year that he and Sarah were expecting their first child and expressed their joy.

In June 2015, the couple announced the birth of their first child, with Chad revealing the news on Twitter, tweeting:

“Sry This year, I’m missing the @JimKellyInc charity in Buffalo. My favorite occasion to attend, but we now have a kid. God’s most precious gift. Sarah and I are overjoyed and plan to spend many days enjoying family activities and watching @buffalobills football! & also @Chargers right now.”

Chad and Sarah had their second child in March 2017, which he revealed on Instagram with a photo of the infant gripping his finger. The caption read:

“Don’t worry, little girl, you can grip as tightly as you want for as long as you want because I’m already yours and I’ll never let go.” I now have two amazing ladies in my life. My kid and I are both quite fortunate. @rooeemer.”

In 2019, the couple celebrated Chad’s 38th birthday by posting a photo of the complete family on Instagram. Chad said that he had gotten a lot of thanks and affection and that he appreciated everyone’s birthday greetings. He went on to say:

“More movies, shows, and stories to share in the future.” Thank you to my beautiful wife @rooeeemer for always making my life better. Because of you and our tiny kids, it’s more full, concentrated, and my heart bursts with delight.”

Chad and Sarah have continued to offer glimpses of their love on various social media platforms. The pair are also planning a trip to Canada in 2021. With a third kid on the way, the couple is still going strong as they prepare to be parents for the third time.

Chad Michael Murray is best known for his role as Lucas Scott in the 131-episode drama series One Tree Hill. He also appeared in 14 episodes of the ABC series Agent Carter as Jack Thompson. Sarah Roemer, meanwhile, made her feature debut in 2006 with Wristcutters: A Love Story and The Grudge 2. She rose to prominence for her roles as Leila Buchanan in NBC’s The Event and Avery Sharp in the action-thriller series Chosen.
