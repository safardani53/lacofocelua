The highly-anticipated ‘Vanderpump Rules’ reunion filmed on Mar. 23, 2023, nearly three weeks after cast members Tom Sandoval and Ariana Madix called it quits on their nine-year relationship. As pop-culture followers know, the pair broke up due to Tom’s affair with their co-star Raquel Leviss, who has since apologized for hurting Ariana. Since the bombshell rocked Bravo fans everywhere, many viewers, including the other cast members have since reacted to the ‘Scandoval’ situation.

A few of their co-stars who were spotted arriving at the set of the reunion, who also reacted to their breakup, include Lala Kent, Scheana Shay, Katie Maloney, and more. Those three ladies have appeared on social media, podcasts, and other platforms to dish about Tom and Raquel’s affair, with Katie who said that Raquel ‘deserves’ the backlash she has received.
