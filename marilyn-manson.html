Marilyn Manson, American Musician, Actor

blog

Marilyn Manson (né Brian Hugh Warner) is an American musician and actor. He rose to fame in the 1990s with his eponymous rock band, which topped the Billboard 200 with 1998's Mechanical Animals and 2003's The Golden Age of Grotesque. Manson has also appeared on FX's Sons of Anarchy, Showtime's Californiacation, and HBO's Eastbound & Down.
