Everything there is to know about Huma Abedin

Her parents, Syed Zaynul Abedin and Saleha, moved to Saudi Arabia shortly after and resided for many years before returning to the United States.

Her siblings are Hassan, Mahmoud, and Hiba. Syed, Huma’s father, founded the Institute of Muslim Minority Affairs in London and Saudi Arabia.
