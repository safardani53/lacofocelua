Truth behind Charli DAmelios Leaked Pictures with Chase Hudson

August 26, 2024
blog

It seems like Charli D’Amelio and Chase Hudson (Lil Huddy) are back together. 

The speculation started after leaked pictures of the couple started trending on social media.

While D’Amelio and Hudson have not confirmed their relationship yet, fans argue the pictures speak for themselves. 

Charli D’Amelio and Chase Hudson’s Relationship

D’Amelio and Hudson reportedly dated in 2019 but made their relationship public at the beginning of 2020.

However, in the April of the same year, they announced their breakup. 

Regardless, the two were on good terms — they were often pictured hanging out together. Similarly, D’Amelio has been very supportive of Hudson throughout his endeavors. 

Charli D’Amelio and Chase Hudson embracing each other. (Source: Instagram)

One such instance is when she featured as Hudson’s love interest for his music video, ‘America’s Sweetheart.’

Similarly, the two also attended the iHearRadio Music Awards on May 27, 2021. 

All this time, the two said nothing about their relationship, other than that they were just friends.

D’Amelio also attended the launch party of his debut album, Teenage Heartbreak, on September 17, 2021.

She gifted him a purple cake with white icing that said, “Sorry I broke your heart, but at least I gave you something to write about.” 

She posted a couple of pictures on her Instagram stories from the party. 

In October 2021, Hudson went on a getaway with D’Amelio’s parents and Dixie and Noah Beck. They went to Italy and shared photos of their vacation on their respective social media. 

Hudson and D’Amelio also celebrated Halloween together. They wore matching costumes inspired by Alice in Wonderland and The Nightmare Before Christmas. 

Leaked pictures of Charli D’ Amelio and Chase Hudson. (Source: tiktokinsiders' Instagram)

Are Charli D’Amelio and Chase Hudson Back Together?

The TikTokers’ fans were sure that the two were back together during that time. Half of them believed this, whereas the other half said they were just friends. 

But now, some photos from Hudson’s party have leaked that have convinced the fans that the pair are back together. It is of the couple hugging and kissing each other. 

The pictures have been circulating everywhere and are trending.

Trending hashtags. (Source: tiktokinsiders' Instagram)

It is said that the pictures were accidentally leaked by the photo booth company that was present at the party. However, they deleted it quickly, but all in vain. 

Similarly, hashtags #textsforcharli and #textsforhudson are also trending. Some fans said positive hashtags to support the pair and distract them from the leaked images. 

Neither D’Amelio nor Hudson has said anything about the leaked pictures. Therefore, everything is merely an assumption unless the two confirm they are back together.
