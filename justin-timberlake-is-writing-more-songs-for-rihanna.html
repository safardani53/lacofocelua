Two of the hottest names in modern music are trying to collaborate for a second time around – but will JT’s tracks make the cut?

Justin Timberlake, 29, knows a good thing when he sees one, which is why the singer/producer is laying down tracks for Rihanna’s fifth studio album.

“Justin is really eager to work with Rihanna again. He’s written a bunch of new songs and wants to executive produce her next album,” a music industry insider confides to HollywoodLife.com. “He thinks a collaboration would ensure a lot of #1 hits, so has already emailed about ten songs to Ri.”

The two have already worked together on the song Cold Case Love off of Rihanna’s latest CD, R-Rated, which dropped last November. In fact, the 22-year-old Bajan beauty is such a fan of the track that she’s pushing to make it her next single.

“Rihanna is a huge fan of Justin’s as well, and is ready to work with again, as well,” says our source. “So watch for the new album by the last quarter of the year.”

Dope. Our fingers are crossed that the collaborative efforts will be a success – but how could they not be?
