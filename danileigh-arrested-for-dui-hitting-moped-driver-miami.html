DANILEIGH was arrested during Memorial Day weekend after police say she did a hit and run.

The ex and baby mama of rapper DaBaby was arrested on Tuesday morning in Miami Beach after police say she allegedly hit a moped rider with her car while she was driving under the influence, according to police reports obtained by TMZ.

Many bystanders saw the singer driving very fast while swerving in and out of lanes.

The police officer that pulled her over said she smelled like alcohol and failed field sobriety tests.

According to the officer, she blew 0.145 and 0.148 on a breathalyzer test, which is way over the legal limit.

MORE ABOUT DANILEIGH

DaniLeigh, full name Danielle Leigh Curiel, is a musician and dancer.

She began her career as a backup dancer and choreographer for other performers including Pharrell Williams and Nelly Furtado.

She started posting covers of songs on YouTube, then moved to Los Angeles from Florida.

Her breakthrough came after working with Prince on the music video for Breakfast Can Wait.

She began creating her own RnB music after she signed with a record label in 2017.

DANILEIGH'S RELATIONSHIP WITH DABABY

DaniLeigh and DaBaby worked together when she choreographed his music video for BOP on Broadway at the end of 2019.

DaBaby then featured on DaniLeigh’s single, Levi High.

Rumors about the couple's relationship status have been going on all year, and they particularly amped up when the mother of DaBaby's daughter said they were together.

They also were seen together during quarantine and DaBaby wrote explicit song lyrics about her while naming her in his music.

The couple didn't make any comments on their relationship at the time.

Then, in September 2020, there were reports that they'd called off their romance.

DaniLeigh posted a number of cryptic posts, one reading: "I never had HATERS til I dated u," which followers assumed was about DaBaby.

However, in December 2020 it seemed things were back on as DaniLeigh posted a picture of them together with her arms around the fellow rapper.

She captioned the post with "My baby idc".

IT'S A GIRL!
