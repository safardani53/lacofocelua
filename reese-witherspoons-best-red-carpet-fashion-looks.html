Reese Witherspoons Best Red Carpet Fashion Looks

March 12, 2024
blog

Mini dresses! Gowns! Sequins! Reese Witherspoon is nothing short of a style icon and knows how to command attention on the red carpet.

The Big Little Lies star has done it all — from winning an academy award to founding her lifestyle brand Draper James and writing Whiskey in a Teacup: What Growing Up in the South Taught Me About Life, Love, and Baking Biscuits.

When she took home the Oscar for Best Leading Actress in 2006, the southern belle wore a 1955 vintage Christian Dior dress that was a thing of dreams. It featured an embroidered floral design, V-neck, and A-line skirt. She topped it off with a glamorous updo, bright pink lips and flirty eyelashes. At the 2014 Met Gala she channeled her Legally Blonde character Elle Woods and rocked a sleek hot pink Stella McCartney gown that included a bodycon fit and strapless neckline.

The Your Place or Mine star doesn’t only look fabulous in gowns, but she rocks suits just as well. At the June 2022 Where the Crawdads Sing photocall, the Home Again actress brought the drama in a lavender ensemble by Victoria Beckham. She teamed the chic getup with a white blouse underneath, side part and thin gold hoops. And at the 2017 Emmys, she turned the heat up even more with a navy-blue Stella McCartney jacket dress that showed off her toned limbs.

One of her most stylish looks came at the February 2023 premiere of Daisy Jones & The Six. For the soirée, she stunted a Schiaparelli midi dress. The frock was embellished with standout gold buttons lining the front and finished with a sweetheart neckline. The Four Christmases actress looked effortlessly elegant with a deep side part twisted into a slicked back updo, diamond earrings and soft glam.

The Morning Show star wore another Schiaparelli garb at the 2022 Surface premiere. On the red carpet, Witherspoon beamed in a white mini dress with sparkly gold details sewn into the front of the number. The extravagant beading was placed underneath her bust, on her waist and cascaded from her hips to the end of the frock.

The This Means War actress rocked the sleeveless piece with matching heels, a dainty ring and messy updo. For makeup, the Louisiana native sported a dewy face, eyeliner, long lashes and glossy lips.
