KIM Kardashian has shared a video of her youngest son, Psalm, on her social media account looking vastly different than he usually does.

Psalm West didn't have his soft curls in the clip that his mom Kim uploaded onto her recent Instagram Stories.

The Skims founder, 43, filmed the four-year-old as he stood on the sidelines of his 8-year-old brother Saint's recent soccer practice in Los Angeles, California.

Kim centered her camera on Psalm's face to flaunt the toddler's new look.

Instead of having a head full of hair, the youngster rocked a buzzcut.

"Does anybody recognize this boy?," Kim can be heard asking in the background.
