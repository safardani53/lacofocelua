KARDASHIAN fans have taken to social media to beg Kim to stop doing her signature photo pose.

Online critics mocked Kim's notable kissy-face pose and called for her to "stop" doing it after the reality star struck the pose in new pics she shared on Instagram.

The photos that the Skims founder shared were identical pics, except one of them appeared to be taken with flash and the other without.

The 42-year-old was wearing her hair slicked back in a braid in the pics; however the braid was only visible in one snap, and she sported a snakeskin bustier.

Kim's makeup was soft glam in the photos and her cleavage nearly spilled out of her skintight top.

Since the Hulu personality's face was close-up in the camera, so was her plump lip kiss face.

Many fans slammed Kim's go-to photo pose, expressing in a Reddit forum that it's time for her to update it.

'SO REPETITIVE'

"Let go of this pose! everything is so .. outdated .. the tight ponytail, the makeup, the pose. So repetitive," one person criticized in the forum.

One person replied jokingly: "Imagine scrolling through her phone and there are like 700+ kissy face photos lol"

Another person ranted: "She looks beautiful, the boobs are fantastic, but holy F**K the duck face was retired in 2012."
