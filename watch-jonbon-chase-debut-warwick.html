Jonbon into 2-1 for the Arkle after thrashing Monmiral in hugely impressive debut over fences at War

July 04, 2024
blog

JONBON has been cut into 2-1 for the Arkle after a hugely impressive debut over fences.

The £570,000 full brother to 2016 Arkle champ Douvan hardly put a foot wrong when thrashing the highly-rated Monmiral by seven lengths at Warwick.

Paddy Power and Betfair cut the Nicky Henderson-trained superstar from 7-2 to just 2-1 for success at next year's Festival.

Winning jockey Aidan Coleman said: "Very straightforward. Jumped very well and won nicely, it's great.

"Monmiral is a very good horse and we were only a few pounds clear of him in ratings

"You wouldn't have thought it would be the one-horse race people were making out it would be but we won well.

"It's everything I wanted to see.

"He walked round the preliminaries super, went down to start easy and jumped super, we're very happy."

Trainer Hendo said: "This was always going to be his game but he was a good hurdler last year - only one better than him.
