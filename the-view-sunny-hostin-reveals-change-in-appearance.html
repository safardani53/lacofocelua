THE View personality Sunny Hostin has unveiled her new sassy accessory on social media.

Sunny's latest Wednesday Instagram Stories exhibited her new ear piercing among the plethora of her other piercings.

The 54-year-old TV host's video revolved around her giving the deets to fans who inquired about where she got the earrings she tends to wear on The View.

"So I've been getting so many questions from viewers, I figured that I had to post, yes I'm completely obsessed with Maria Tash," Sunny said as she pulled her hair back to expose her multi-pierced ear adorned with jewels.

Maria Tash is a New York-based luxury piercing and jewelry brand.

The morning show moderator confessed that all of the several stud earrings along her ear came from Maria Tash.
