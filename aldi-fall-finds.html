I'm an Aldi fan - 11 fall finds starting at 55 cents, including cute pumpkin-shaped candles

February 09, 2024
blog

AN Aldi superfan has taken to TikTok to show off some of her favorite fall finds from her local store.

TikTok user AldiAllTheTime used her platform to let her followers in on what is and isn't worth buying at Aldi.

The TikToker, whose real first name is Sarah, regularly shares Aldi finds, reviews and recipes on her account.

The influencer used her post to take her over 200,000 followers on a trip through the aisles of her local Aldi to show off all the fall finds she recommends.

Below is what Sarah recommended shoppers keep an eye out for during their next trip to Aldi.

Just keep in mind availability and prices will vary by location.

Here are all the fall items Sarah suggested keeping an eye out for.

1. Donuts

The first item on Sarah's list were the apple cider donuts, which can be seen in the video listed at $5.89

2. Pumpkin loaf cake

Next up was a package of pumpkin loaf cake, which Instacart lists at $3.85.

Sarah said, "This is delicious. Definitely try it."

3. Bagels

After that, Sarah showed three different kinds of bagels: Maple French Toast, Harvest, and Pumpkin Spice.

The French toast variety is listed at $4.39 on Instacart, while the Pumpkin and Harvest options are $2.19.

4. Creamer

Next Sarah showed a few different creamer options.

There was a limited-edition Pumpkin Spice Almond Milk and a regular Pumpkin Spice creamer which sell on Instacart for $4.35.

There's also a Chobani Oat Pumpkin Spice creamer for $3.99 which Sarah said is "really good".

5. Yogurt

Aldi is stocking seasonal yogurts in maple, apple and cinnamon, and pumpkin pie.

Sarah said, "I love these fall-themed yogurts. I buy them every year."

She also pointed out that Two Good brand is out with a pumpkin yogurt.

Instacart lists the Aldi yogurts at 55 cents, but the Two Good brand shown in the video is not listed on Instacart.

6. Bottled coffee

Up next, Sarah shared two different flavors of bottled coffee: Pumpkin Spice Latte and Vanilla Chai.

Each at $6.05 on Instacart.

7. Pizza

Sarah then showed a harvest themed frozen pizza, which was not listed on Instacart.

8. Cheesecake sampler

Sarah said the cheesecake sampler looks "amazing".

The price was not listed on Instacart, but they do have the individual flavors available for $2.65 each.

9. Dessert hummus

Next the influencer said she does not normally like dessert hummus, but they are "really popular".

She showed two varieties: sea salt caramel and pumpkin pie.

The prices were not listed on Instacart.

10. Candles

Sarah took a break from food to show off some candles which she pointed out "were a lot nicer than the ones they usually have".

The pumpkin shaped candles are $7.99 on Instacart.

There are also three-wick candles in seasonal scents: amber sunrise, juniper suede, apple prosecco and cinnamon pumpkin latte.

11. Plates

Last but not least Sarah showed off some fun, fall-themed decorative plates which she thought were "adorable".

The prices were not listed on Instacart.
