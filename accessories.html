Thanksgiving is almost approaching, so it’s the right time to start planning what type of hat you will wear to the family dinner or late-night parties. The Thanksgiving turkey hat is ideal for any event and is an amazing way to spice up your rather boring Thanksgiving meal. But choosing the right turkey hat from a plethora of choices can be overwhelming. We’ve put together this detailed buying guide to help you choose the perfect turkey hat.

Factors To Consider When Buying a Turkey Hat

Your outfit

You should first decide how you want to dress up for Thanksgiving. If you want to be in the limelight of any gathering, choose a turkey hat that’s both stylish and comfy. You can choose a simple turkey hat if you want to go for a minimalist look, or go the opposite route with a fun and show-stopping option.

Material

Wool turkey hats are stylish and cozy, but they aren’t an ideal choice for sensitive skin people. Although fleece hats are really warm and won't irritate your skin, they might not appear as classy as wool turkey hats.

Your head’s size

While choosing a turkey hat, measure your head’s size to ensure that the hat is of the right size. You don’t want to end up with a hat that’s either too tight to irritate your skin and scalp or too loose that it keeps falling off your head. If you aren’t sure of the size, it’s better to purchase a turkey hat from your nearby store so that you can try the hat on.

Turkey hat’s design

Turkey hats come in different designs including classic designs with just the face of the turkey, and more fun and quirky designs. Be sure to purchase the hat that complements your entire outfit.

Different Styles of Turkey Hats

Silly hats

An entertaining and creative way to commemorate Thanksgiving is by purchasing goofy hats. These hats have a turkey resting on the head with two long legs that work as ear flaps. You can find hats that have different goofy expressions on the turkey’s face, adding a fun element to your entire outfit. You can wear this type of turkey hat at a gathering or when you're enjoying games with your family or friends.

Beanies

Beanies are a winter apparel staple to keep yourself toasty and warm. And because Thanksgiving is purely a winter celebration, getting a beanie hat is an excellent choice to keep chilly winds at bay. The perk of getting a beanie hat is that it comes in a standard size that fits all and is somewhat stretchy to offer a snug fit. Beanies will be a hit whether you purchase them for yourself or give them as a gift to a loved one.

Thanksgiving headband

If you don’t want to mess up your hairstyle, wearing a headband is a wonderful way to celebrate Thanksgiving. You can find a range of designs, but most of them will have a turkey and other motifs like pumpkins to spice up your Thanksgiving outfit.

The perk of choosing headbands over hats is that they are versatile and look fantastic with almost any type of attire. Plus, you can also wear it to your workplace or school. For anyone who enjoys the Holiday season, it would make a wonderful present or stocking stuffer.

How Do You Take Head Measurements for a Turkey Hat?

You must take measurements of your head to pick the right size for your Turkey hat. Follow these steps to determine your ideal hat size:

  • Take a measuring tape and wrap it around your head's widest point.
  • Measure from the middle of your forehead to the top of your head. Divide this number by two and round it off to the nearest whole number.
  • Compare it to the size chart available.
  • If you're in between sizes, go with the smaller one for a snug fit.
