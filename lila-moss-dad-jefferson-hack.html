LILA Moss is best known as the daughter of British supermodel Kate Moss.

But it’s not just her mother who’s made a name for herself, as her father Jefferson is pretty famous too.

Who is Lila Moss's dad Jefferson Hack?

Jefferson Hack was born in Montevideo, Uruguay in 1941, before moving to Kent as a young child.

He is a curator, creative director and co-founder of Dazed Media – an independent media company.

The company’s portfolio includes high profile bi annual magazines including Another Magazine and Another Man.

As a journalist and media entrepreneur, Hack has worked as a creative consultant with a string of high profile stars including Bjork and U2.

In 2015, he also collaborated with Rihanna and fashion designer Alexander McQueen on a ground breaking project - resulting in the world’s first digital cover magazine.

Hack, who has a reported net worth of $50million, is also the co-founder of global video channel Nowness.

Why did Kate Moss and Jefferson Hack split?

Following a high profile relationship with rock guitarist Antony Langdon, Moss began dating Hack in the early 2000s.

In September 2002, the couple welcomed their daughter Lila Grace, but decided to split two years later.

Reports suggest they both continued to spend time with their daughter who, like her parents, has embarked on a career in fashion.

Lila landed her first major campaign with Marc Jacobs Beauty at the age of just 16.

Ever since, she has also modelled for brands such as Fendi and Versace and The Braid Bar.

At the time of writing, Lila has 330k followers on Instagram, where she posts photos of all her most recent projects and even advertises the fact that she is signed by her mother's agency: Kate Moss Agency.

However, despite her parents’ A-list status, Hack told Sunday Times Magazine that Lila finds them "deeply uncool."
