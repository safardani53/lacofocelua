Sophie Turner is one of Hollywood’s favorite young stars, known for her dramatic chops on-screen and he wry sense of humor when off. The British beauty, born Feb 21. 1996, soared to fame with her very first acting gig at age 15, playing Sansa Stark on the HBO saga ‘Game Of Throne’ from 2011 to 2019. She earned a Primetime Emmy nomination for the role in 2019. Sophie is also part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe, appearing as Jean Grey in ‘Dark Phoenix’ and ‘X-Men: Apocalypse. Sophie wed middle Jonas Brother, Joe, during a quickie ceremony in Las Vegas in May 2019. They made things more formal almost two months later, inviting friends to a romantic wedding in Paris. The couple shares a daughter named Willa, born July 2020 and another child, born Jul. 2022. The couple split in September 2023.
