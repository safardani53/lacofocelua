YOUTUBE star JoJo Siwa has showcased the enormous mansion she shares with her family in LA thanks to her $10million fortune.

The 16-year-old took to her channel where she gave a tour of the palatial pad having recently moved from her former home.

JoJo, real name Joelle Jonie Siwa, shared snap shots of the colourful interiors including the rainbow headboard on her bed as well as her famously shiny ensembles kept on display in glass cabinets.

And as the singer and former Dance Moms star gives a tour of the exterior fans will be able to spot her custom BMW parked out front

When you first enter the house guests are treated to a display of JoJo’s most famous outfits which are set up around the family’s grand piano.

She then takes fans into her “fun room”where there is an enormous buffet of sweets, as well as a nacho, slush and popcorn machine.

The shelves are also stocked up with her namesake JoJo Juice for any one feeling thirsty.

Next up is what she calls “the merch room” containing shelves and shelves of the official JoJo Siwa products.

As she tours the room, the YouTuber says: “This is like an actually store.

“In our last house we had the merch warehouse which was in our garage and then we had the merch room which was in our house.

“Now that JoJo merch has been taken to the completely bigger level, there’s like every product under the sun and the moon and the stars and the galaxy and the whole milkyway we were like we need a place to actually store all this.

“It’s amazing here.”

After a quick tour of the family kitchen and a VERY high tech security system JoJo then gives her 10 million subscribers a tour of the yard, which includes a basketball court, pool and foosball table.

JoJo says: “The one thing I actually love about this house is that it has an actual yard, most houses in LA don’t have any actual outdoor space because they’re all really close together.”

The star shared a second video where she gave a tour of her master ensuite bedroom.

She gave a tour of one of her two walk-in wardrobes as well as the wall she has dedicated to her signature JoJo bows and her rainbow headboard.

Born in Nebraska on May 19, 2003, the 16-year-old has since moved to Los Angeles

JoJo rose to fame as a castmember on hit reality show Dance Moms between 2015 to 2016.

The show follows a dance troupe led by owner and chief choreographer, Abby Lee Miller, as they compete against their rivals in national competitions.
