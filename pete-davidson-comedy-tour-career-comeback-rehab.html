PETE Davidson has decided to hit the road with two other must-see comedians.

Pete, 29, sold out two shows in Flordia for his show titled  Pete Davidson Working Out New Material with Friends.

These were the first two shows after he entered rehab to seek treatment for his mental health issues.

This was after the Saturday Night Live comedian was seen at Thick & Thin Pizza, a family-owned pizzeria located in Boca Raton, Florida.

Now, Pete is headlining a comeback tour and partnering up with Jon Stewart, 60, and John Mulaney, 40.

The tour is called Jon, John & Pete, and it will be a limited run between September 8 and 10.
