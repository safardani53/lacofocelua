A YOUNG woman who works at ASOS’ head office has lifted the lid on what it’s really like at one of the biggest fashion retailers in the world. 

Siyana Zak revealed that fashion and beauty brands visit and give them freebies, staff get their make-up done during work time and the food options border on gourmet. 

The marketing professional has been working at the e-commerce giant for three months after graduating university. 

Siyana took to social media to share behind the scenes pictures of her job. 

She said: “I love coming in to this.”

Among the companies she has experienced “brand talks” from are Levi’s, New Balance and Dr. Martens.

Bosses at ASOS also organised a face and body pop-up event where big-name beauty brands visited to give their staff freebies.

These include Urban Decay, Nars, MAC and Too Faced.

What’s more, Siyana has had her make-up done by the experts at Doll Beauty and her hair done by stylists at GHD during a normal working day. 

The modern office is decorated with foliage, a glass ceiling and comfy sofas - which Siyana dubbed “cool” and said she was “obsessed with”.

ASOS’ head office is based in Camden, London in a building that used to be a cigarette factory.

Bosses at the retailer said: “Now, the iconic Art Deco building has been completely renovated with spacious office areas, ASOS studios (where we shoot all our products), beauty rooms and a free gym.

“There’s also a subsidised canteen and cafe.”

Snaps that Siyana shared of the food were fluffy pancakes on Pancake Day, pizzas, chicken burgers and street food-esque grub. 

People were blown away by her pictures and asked her to share how she got into their “dream” role.

One person said: “Damn, I need this job.”

A second quipped: “How do I sign up?

"Longest running customer, not gonna lie.”
