Kim Kardashian and Paris Hilton reunited at the reality TV star’s Christmas party bash is making our hearts melt. It all started when Kim began working as Paris’ closet organizer. Now, they are enjoying their riches and fame together – by sledding down the fake snow all over Kim’s mansion.

Kim Kardashian and Paris Hilton: Christmas party

Kim Kardashian and Paris Hilton were reunited at the infamous Christmas Eve party. They went sledding together, posed for pictures in front of the huge tree, and “served Winter Wonderland looks.”

Paris shared old photos of the two doing the exact same a few years ago, when she wore a sparkly red dress and Kim had a short white get-up on. This time, they both went super glam in long ball gowns.

She commented on Kim’s post, “What an epic night!🥳 Love you sis🥰😍.” Paris’ Instagram highlight even shows snippets of her with Kim, telling her friend she “loves her.” Come on, they were “sliving”…

Paris originally hired Kim as closet girl

Kim Kardashian got her first real taste of fame when she organised Paris Hilton’s closet. This was after having gone through the closet of the likes of Cindy Crawford, Serena Williams, and Brandy.

Later, Kim became Hilton’s assistant, then, her friend. They started showing up on red carpets together and have both gone onto become billionaires, with both their families heavily in the public eye.

The Hulu star was actually friends with Paris in pre-school! Their friendship was first documented on Hilton and Nicole Richie’s reality show, The Simple Life, which began airing in 2003.
