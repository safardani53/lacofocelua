Subway announces it's giving away free sandwiches to celebrate 'complex' change - how to claim yours

August 25, 2024
blog

SUBWAY has announced it is giving away free sandwiches to celebrate a complex change in its operations.

In an ongoing effort to change its menu and improve guest experience, the fast food chain has introduced four new sandwiches.

Subway has announced four new sandwiches as part of its Deli Heroes collection.

The sandwiches can be ordered by name and number as part of the chain's Subway Series menu.

To facilitate the menu change, Subway has introduced one of its most complex changes ever by installing deli meat slicers in 20,000 of its restaurants.

The new sandwiches on the Deli Heroes menu all utilize the new meat slicers.

READ MORE ON SUBWAY

The Titan Turkey, Grand Slam Ham, Garlic Roast Beef, and the Beast all feature fresh meats and cheeses on Subway's bread.

To celebrate the milestone, Subway is offering up to 1million free six-inch Deli Heroes subs at restaurants around the country.

On July 11 between 10am and noon, the first 50 customers at participating locations can claim their free sub.

The free sandwiches are only available in-restaurant.

“Over the past two years, we overhauled our expansive pantry of ingredients and debuted a whole new way to Subway with chef-crafted signature sandwiches," Trevor Haynes, President, North America at Subway, said in a statement.

"These major changes led to rave reviews from our guests and record-breaking sales.

“This year’s changes are even bigger and more transformational. The addition of freshly sliced meats is the most impactful yet as it gives our guests a better sandwich – raising the bar even higher for the brand that defined fresh.

"We can’t wait for America to taste the difference and see how far we have come on our journey.”
