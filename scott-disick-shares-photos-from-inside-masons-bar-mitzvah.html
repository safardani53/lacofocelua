Scott Disick Shares Photos From Inside Mason's Bar Mitzvah

February 13, 2024
blog

A feast fit for a king — or a teen! Scott Disick gave fans a peek at son Mason‘s bar mitzvah, which featured several food trucks offering an array of snacks.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum, 39, shared photos from the lavish bash via his Instagram Story on Monday, December 19. “Thanks @RaisingCanes for the delicious food!” the reality star captioned one snap of chicken fingers and French fries.

Additional photos showed Van Leeuwen ice cream and churros from The Churro Man. Disick also posted several photos of the decor, which included silver balloons and a large sign reading, “Mason’s Bar Mitzvah December 2022.”

The Flip It Like Disick alum’s eldest son turned 13 on Wednesday, December 14, and celebrated his bar mitzvah at Offsunset lounge in West Hollywood on Saturday, December 17. Mason’s mom, Kourtney Kardashian, was in attendance at the soirée along with the other two children she and Disick share: Penelope, 10, and Reign, 8.

While photographs showed Kardashian, 43, arriving with Penelope and Disick walking in with Reign, the Poosh founder’s husband, Travis Barker, seemingly wasn’t present. The Hulu personality and the Blink-182 drummer, 47, tied the knot in May at a glamorous ceremony in Italy.

While Barker seemed to be missing from the festivities, Mason’s aunts Kim Kardashian, Khloé Kardashian, Kendall Jenner and Kylie Jenner were all in attendance, as was his grandma Kris Jenner. The Good American cofounder, 38, shared a few snaps from the event, posing with nieces Penelope and North, 9, whom Kim, 41, shares with ex-husband Kanye West.

“Mazel Tov Mason!!!” Khloé captioned the candid photos. “The girls and I attempted to take some pics.”

Mason and his brother, Reign, share the same birthday, which several of their family members celebrated by sharing adorable throwback photos via social media. “December 14th… one of the best days of my life, twice and forever. my birthday boys ❤️❤️,” Kourtney wrote via Instagram, sharing a picture of a younger Mason holding an infant Reign.

The wellness guru and Barker were previously trying to conceive a child together via in vitro fertilization, but Kourtney revealed in September that they put the process on pause to “focus” on their wedding.
