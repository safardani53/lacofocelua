A BEAUTY guru has shared the top five hacks she’s discovered to keep her youthful looks and remain wrinkle-free.

Taking to social media, Nicole told viewers that she’s just weeks away from turning 39 and will be 40 in just a year. 

“And I have zero wrinkles,” she shared. “Here are my top tier secrets. Top tier, top shelf.

“Just so you know, I do not have any Botox - never had it before - and I’ve never had filler in my face other than my lips.”

She then jumped into her list of hacks, with the first being to wear sunscreen every single day. 

Nicole said: “Without sounding like a broken record, wearing your SPF. I’ve been wearing SPF since my early 20s and let me tell you, the sun is the number one cause of premature and early ageing.”

Next on her list of hacks is to use a retinoid product. She said it’s “so important” to start doing this in your early to mid 20s as it’s great for acne, fine lines and wrinkles.
