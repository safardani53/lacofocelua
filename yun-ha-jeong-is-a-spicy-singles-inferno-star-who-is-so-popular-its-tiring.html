Singles Inferno season 3 brings a whole new format to Netflix this year. The participants are heading off to Paradise almost immediately, and fans are loving the “exciting” cast. Yun Ha-Jeong is one of the season’s contestants unafraid to speak her mind.

The Korean dating show has amped up the flirtation for Single’s Inferno season 3. The pool scenes are still in full flow, but there’s certainly a new added element of spiciness to this year’s reality TV show. Singles Inferno‘s Ha-Jeong gets more than one vote from her co-stars on Inferno, meaning she had super high chances of being whisked off to Paradise.

Meet Yun Ha-Jeong

After the Single’s Inferno big season 3 twist, viewers find out there are more contestants living on another island.

Season 3 episode 2 sees fans introduced to Yun Ha-Jeong. She describes herself as very popular person. She adds people often call her a “sly fox of a woman.”

When it comes to Ha-Jeong’s popularity, she says it can actually get “tiring” at times.

Oozing confidence, the Netflix star doubts there’ll be another girl on the show “with a prettier smile” than hers, adding: “Especially these dimples.”

Ha-Jeong is 26 years old and explains in episode 2 she works in an office for a clothing brand.

Singles Inferno star’s ‘look’ to catch attention

Since season 3 dropped on Netflix, fans have been taking to social media and Reddit threads to comment on the cast members.

It looks like Ha-Jeong has bagged top spot for the show’s most “toxic” star but fans are here for it.

She’s been dubbed “confident” and “entertaining” by viewers who are loving the “spiciness.”

Singles Inferno host, Dex (Kim Jin-young), points out Ha-Jeong seems to have a “look” she does in order to catch her fellow contestants’ attention.

While some fans find it “cringey,” others don’t mind her gaze, writing: “It reminds me of how in cartoons, the female character will pull her chin back and bat her eye lashes to seduce the male character lolololol.”
