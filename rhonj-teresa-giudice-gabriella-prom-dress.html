RHONJ star Teresa Giudice’s daughter Gabriella, 17, has shown off her stunning emerald dress for her junior prom night.

Fans gushed that she looked “so grown up” in the beautiful gown.

As her 17-year-old daughter Gabriella prepared for her junior prom, Teresa, 50, bought in the best of the best and hired celebrity makeup artist Priscilla DiStasio to do Gabriella’s glam.

Priscilla shared two videos showing off the teen’s breathtaking prom night look.

Gabriella looked a million dollars as she wore an emerald green fishtail gown with a sweep train.

She accessorized with silver feather earrings and a matching bracelet.

Gabriella is one of the four children that Teresa shares with her ex Joe Giudice along with Gia, Milania, 16; and Audriana, 13.

‘SO SMART’

Last month, the proud mom revealed the exciting career plans that the soon-to-be high school graduate has for her future.

“At first, she wanted to be a CIA agent, and then she saw how much money they made, and now she’s changed her mind,” Teresa told Bravo’s The Daily Dish.

“We have to start looking at colleges," she added.

“I can’t wait to see what college she goes to, because she is my smartest kid.”

RSVP

But before she heads off to continue her studies, Gabriella will be preparing for her role as a bridesmaid at her mom and 48-year-old Luis Ruelas’ wedding.

Earlier this week, RHONY’s Ramona Singer leaked important details about the family occasion, spilling the date, venue, and the guest list.

The wedding bells will ring for Teresa and Luis on Saturday 8 August, and the venue is the Park Chateau Estate in East Brunswick, New Jersey.

It’s set to be a formal affair, with black tie attire required.
