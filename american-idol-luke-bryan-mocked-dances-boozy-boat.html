American Idol's Luke Bryan mocked by fans as he dances on a boat in wild boozy video

October 05, 2024

AMERICAN Idol judge Luke Bryan has been mocked by fans for his dancing skills.

The country singer, 46, showed off his best moves while drinking a beer and singing along to his new song, Beer In My Hand, on a boat.

Luke wore a white T-shirt with khaki swimming trunks, a khaki hat and white sneakers in the clip.

He clutched his phone in one hand, which blared out his song on full volume.

The American Idol star nodded his head in time to the music as he sang along to the verse: "But I got a beer in my hand!"

Luke then walked around the boat while enthusiastically dancing and told fans: "Coming out tomorrow!"

He posted the video on Instagram on Thursday and captioned it: "Is it Friday yet?"

Fans flooded the comments to poke fun at the Crash My Party hitmaker's dance moves.

One commented, "You dance like your mom," and another added: "Woah this is a lot," with a string of laughing face emojis.

A third posted: "You about to stumble right off that boat… with your beer!"

Others praised Luke's energetic clip, with one enthusing: "You are the best!! You have the happiest personality that makes so many of us so happy!!!!"

The musician is currently appearing on our screens as a judge on American Idol with Lionel Richie and Katy Perry, while Ryan Seacrest is hosting the show.

Earlier this week Ryan found himself in the firing line with his American Idol co-star, Lionel Richie.

The 73-year-old claimed on Wednesday that show producers were giving Ryan preferential treatment.

Lionel posed outside his trailer with the bike Ryan has on the set of American Idol - despite the stage being just 20 feet away.

"The stage is only 20ft away, and @ryanseacrest has a bike!! Production - I need a bike too!!" he captioned the post.

Ryan immediately jumped in to explain the ride by making a joke about his height.

"Listen, 20ft is far for short legs," the radio personality wrote in the comments.

It comes after Idol viewers urged Ryan to quit the show.
