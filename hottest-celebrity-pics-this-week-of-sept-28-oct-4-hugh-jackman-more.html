Amazon is working with tWitch and Allison Holker! Together, they’re announcing Amazon’s new Shared Alexa Routines – a brand new feature that is available on all Amazon Echo Devices for consumers everywhere! tWtich and Allison released their own original Shared Alexa Routine! Known for their family dancing videos and all of the family fun at home, the dynamic duo shared how they keep the family organized with Shared Routines, especially during these crazy times with back to school in full swing! 

tWitch and Allison Holker shared EXCLUSIVELY with HollywoodLife.com on the feature with tWitch saying, “I think in these very special times of quarantine and home schooling, it is a whole new normal right now. I think one of the things that excited us most about the Shared Alexa Routine was that we can have Alexa help us plan out our day. As you know, we have three kids at various stages of life, we have 12-year old, a 4-year-old and a 10-month-old. So making sure that our days run smoothly, we can always use an extra hand with Alexa’s help we can have reminders on when to wake up for school and when Allison and I can take time to meditate or when it is time for snacktime. We even have built in dance breaks and the greatest thing is that you can have a shareable URL, meaning that we can send our routines out for others to do as well.” Where Allison shared, “You can experience a day the way we experience a day! The features I love don’t just remind us, but they can remind the kids. It just gives a reminder to everyone in the house and understand what the timing of everything is for. It is really fun and enjoyable for everyone. Everyone enjoys it!”
