Drake spends just TWO minutes in London nightclub after being paid 15K to make an appearance

October 09, 2024
blog

DRAKE spent two minutes at a club last night after being paid 15K to make an appearance, The Sun’s Bizarre column can exclusively reveal.

The rapper, 30, who’s currently headlining London’s O2, is seen on CCTV arriving at celeb hotspot Toy Room before leaving moments later.

Canadian star Drake entered the club with an entourage that included three girls at 2:17am – and then exited the venue at 2:19.

He complained to owners about not being given “VIP treatment” following his gig at the O2, so didn’t waste a second before leaving.

A club insider said: “Drake was paid £15K to appear at Toy Room but only stayed for two minutes which must be a record.

“The owner of the venue was seen by party goers shouting in the street when he left, clearly frustrated by the situation.

“Toy Room was heaving – Drake took one look at the crowd and immediately left.

“The exclusive club has since been accused online of breaching data protection for boasting about Drake entering the club and posting the footage.”

Fans were quick to mock the club’s Facebook page for revealing that Drake stayed a mere two minutes in the venue.

David Lorenz commented: “The awkward moment when he leaves two minutes later...”

Tito Fereira Perso added: “In 2:17am out 2:19am and everyone gonna be like ‘Guys what a night.’”

Rihanna partied at the same hot spot on New Year’s Eve but was no where to be seen last night.

Instead the star, who’s dating Jennifer Lopez, 47, was accompanied by three girls as he briefly appeared at the club.

J Lo started dating Drake following her split from backing dancer Casper Smart, her boyfriend of four years, while Drake’s on-off romance with Rihanna is thought to have ended in October.

But Drake risked a telling off from new girlfriend Jen after talking about his “sexy” ex-Rihanna on stage.

The star, who The Sun revealed enjoyed a fling with Madonna, told the crowd at his first UK tour date on Monday: “She’s like the queen of everything.

“If you want your woman to feel sexy you play RiRi records.”
