DWTS host Julianne Hough announces plans to flee to Arizona for major career move away from the show

March 29, 2024
blog

JULIANNE Hough has revealed she's jetting off to Arizona for a major career move in a new post.

The professional dancer joined Dancing with the Stars as a co-host this season alongside Fresh Prince of Bel-Air star Alfonso Ribeiro, 52.

Although Season 32 has only just begun, Julianne is already talking about stepping away from the ballroom.

She posted a video to her Instagram Stories over the weekend announcing her upcoming Kinrgy Retreat in Sedona, Arizona.

In the clip, Julianne described the trip, which takes place November 1 through November 6, as the experience of a lifetime.

She also detailed what participants can expect while encouraging those interested to register.

TAKING OVER

Julianne was a pro dancer on DWTS from 2007 to 2009 and later returned to guest judge for various seasons.

She has now replaced former host Tyra Banks, 49, who left the show after three seasons.

The actress gushed over her first day back in a post following the September 26 premiere.

She showed off her appearance in the photo while wearing a black gown with her blond locks styled in a low bun.

She captioned the post in part: "DWTS premiere was a dream. I can't even begin to explain how surreal it was to be back in the ballroom!

"Congratulations to all the couples for giving it their all week one - can't wait to do it all over again next week!"

Julianne and Alfonso later had a second successful week as co-hosts, despite the Rock of Ages star contracting the flu days earlier.

CO-HOSTS' BOND

They celebrated their smooth start while dining at Craig's in West Hollywood, California, later in the week, where Julianne wore a daring ensemble.

The 35-year-old wore an olive green romper that she left unbuttoned, showing her black bra underneath.

She paired the look with knee-high black boots and her hair in an updo as she walked with her dog in a tan carrier on her shoulder.

Julianne wore the same outfit in an Instagram video she shared of her and Alfonso dancing in the middle of a Los Angeles, California, street.

The duo appeared to be meshing well as they performed their choreography with smiles on their faces.

They ended the clip with Alfonso catching Julianne in his arms for the perfect finish.
