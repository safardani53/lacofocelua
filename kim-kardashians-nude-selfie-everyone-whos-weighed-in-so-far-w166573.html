Kim Kardashian's Nude Selfie: Everyone Whos Weighed in So Far

April 28, 2024
blog

Let Us break it down for you. Early Monday, March 7, Kim Kardashian shared her latest nude selfie via Twitter and Instagram. It didn’t take long for two fellow celebs — Bette Midler and Chloe Grace Moretz — to take issue with the full-frontal snap. Once Kardashian, 35, angrily reacted, well, everybody else reacted! Stars like Chrissy Teigen, Kourtney Kardashian, Bella Thorne, Miley Cyrus and even Kim herself, chimed in on the seemingly never-ending, complex fight on Tuesday, March 8. 

Here, Us Weekly details the nude feud; Check back here for (inevitable!) updates.

Bette Midler

The 70-year-old showbiz legend kicked it off when she tweeted on Monday, “Kim Kardashian tweeted a nude selfie today. If Kim wants us to see a part of her we’ve never seen, she’s gonna have to swallow the camera.” Kim then replied: “Hey @BetteMidler I know it’s past your bedtime but if you’re still up and reading this send nudes #justkidding… hey @BetteMidler I really didn’t want to bring up how you sent me a gift awhile back trying to be a fake friend then come at me #dejavu.”

The Beaches star clapped back on Tuesday afternoon. I never tried to fake friend you,” the Hocus Pocus actress replied to Kardashian. “Looks like anyone can take a selfie but not everyone can take a joke…”

Chloe Grace Moretz

Nineteen-year-old Moretz joined the debate on Monday night by retweeting Midler’s post. She then tweeted directly at Kim about her (many) nude photos. “I truly hope you realize how important setting goals are for young women, teaching them we have so much more to offer than just our bodies,” the If I Stay actress wrote to the reality star, who sniped back: “Let’s all welcome @ChloeGMoretz to twitter, since no one knows who she is. your nylon cover is cute boo.”

While people accused Moretz of slut-shaming Kim, the 30 Rock alum said that was far from the truth. “There’s a huge difference in respecting the platform that you’re given as a celebrity and ‘slut shaming,'” she tweeted. “Something I never have done and would never do.”

Piers Morgan

Kim was keeping up with the British TV star late Monday night, when he mocked her on his show Good Morning Britain. “Hey @piersmorgan never offer to buy a married woman clothes,” the Armenian-American beauty replied. In this case, Kardashian was referring to Morgan’s previous offer to buy her some clothes since Kanye West revealed that he was $53 million in debt. “Thats on some ashley madison type s–t #forresearch.”

Chrissy Teigen

The Sports Illustrated Swimsuit model and Kimye confidante had the perfect response to one of Kardashian’s tweets late Monday. “Sorry I’m late to the party guys,” Kim blasted to her followers. “I was busy cashing my 80 million video game check & transferring 53 million into our joint account.” Twitter-loving Teigen, who’s pals with the reality star and Kanye West, then hilarious replied, “@KimKardashian well iiiiiiiii just ordered thai food.”

Bella Thorne

She has a friend in Bella! Former Disney star Thorne took to Twitter to voice her support for Kim’s naked pics on International Women’s Day, and threw shade at fellow teen star Moretz during the process. “I think every woman should be allowed to make their own choices with their OWN bodies soo..” Thorne, 18, replied. “And it’s not our place to tell them what they can and can’t do with their body ..#internationalwomensday.”

Miley Cyrus

She really couldn’t. Cyrus chimed in on the multiple feuds and sub-feuds on Tuesday, and voiced her disgust with EVERYONE on International Women’s Day. “Dear women, you ALL are acting tacky AF!” Cyrus sniped on Instagram. “Why don’t we overly (myself included) fortunate women come together and try to create and bring jobs to other women in desperate need of them so they can support not only THEMSELVES but their families! . . . Can we all put the c–tiness aside for one f–king day and love / celebrate one another!”
