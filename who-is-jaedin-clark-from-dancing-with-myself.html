Who Is Jaedin Clark From Dancing With Myself?

July 20, 2024
blog

Dancing With Myself is planned to launch on NBC on May 31, 2022, at 10/9c, with several participants attempting to replicate the moves of celebrity founders Nick Jonas, Shakira, and Liza Koshy, as well as presenter Camille Kostek. Jaedin Clark, a Houston athlete who worked as a delivery driver before entering the tournament, is one such contender who will try their luck at winning the public vote and the challenge.

Jaedin Clark in a Dancing With Myself

The 21-year-old is the son of Cedric and Kimberly Clark of Houston, Texas, and has three siblings: Jazmin, Jaxson, and Jordan. The college student graduated from Schaumburg Christian School in 2016, then attended Scottsdale Christian Academy for a year before enrolling at Chaparral High School in 2017. During his education, he participated in Choir and served as Captain of the Men’s Swim Team.

Clark enrolled at Virginia Tech in 2019 after graduating high school and will graduate with a B.S.B.A. in Consumer Studies in 2023. During his freshman year, he competed in a variety of swimming events. He finished second in the 50-yard freestyle at the Ohio State Invitational, sixth in the 100-yard freestyle against George Washington, seventh in the 100-yard freestyle against Queens and Richmond, and seventh in the 100-yard fly against Virginia. At the Virginia Tech Invitational, he finished third in the 100 flies, fourth in the 50 free, and eighth in the 100 free.

Clark finished sixth in the 100 fly and eighth in the 100 free at the Virginia Tech Invitational as a sophomore. He worked as a Creamologist at Creamistry for four months while in high school, “preparing dessert orders for consumers” until October 2017. Following that, he worked for three months as a swim teacher at the Phoenix Swim Club in Paradise Valley, Arizona, before becoming a delivery driver for Postmates Inc. in Scottsdale, Arizona, for ten months.

According to his LinkedIn page, he subsequently worked as a delivery driver for DoorDash in Houston, Texas, for four months, until July 2020, driving “motor vehicles in a safe and efficient way.”

Judges for Dancing With Myself

Contestants on Dancing with Myself must give their all into showing off their talents and become the greatest dancer of the night. Nick Jonas told NBC Insider about the upcoming show:

“The show’s appeal is that we are not seeking the most accurate dancer. We’re searching for the individual that has the most energy, the greatest heart, and the best attitude.”

Nick, the youngest of the Jonas Brothers, formerly served as a judge on the American edition of The Voice. After Shaquille O’Neal left the show, the singer joined the Dancing with Myself panel. Shakira, the show’s executive producer, and three-time Grammy winner will join him on the judging panel. The vocalist of Whenever Wherever has sold over 80 million albums and generated countless hit singles.

Liza Koshy, a YouTube celebrity, will also act as a judge in the dance competition. Liza Koshy’s YouTube channel has 17.4 million followers as of this writing. In 2017, the social media sensation became the quickest individual to gain 17 million YouTube followers. She was also named one of the 25 Most Influential People on the Internet in 2019 by Time Magazine. Watch these judges and Clark on Dancing With Myself on Tuesday night, immediately after America’s Got Talent Season 17’s two-hour debut.
