Planning the perfect Christmas? Snap up a FREE ticket to the Ideal Home Show worth 40 with The Sun

September 29, 2024

LOOKING for tips on how to plan the perfect Christmas?

The Sun is offering you the chance to snap up one of 15,000 pairs of tickets worth £40 to the Ideal Home Show - but hurry, the offer ends tomorrow!

Back for 2016 at Olympia London and Event City Manchester, the Ideal Home Show at Christmas brings you everything you need to prepare your home for the festive season and you could go for free in our great ticket giveaway.

From November 10-13 in Manchester and November 23-27 in London, this annual Christmas shopping event has everything from decorations to hand crafted gifts, Christmas puddings and festive fizz, to furniture and New Year home improvements - the show has it all.

Shop from hundreds of exhibitors across eight sections all under one roof at the bigger home show at Christmas.

The Ideal Home Show at Christmas isn’t just for shopping. It is a great day out for the family, too!

There’s live entertainment, celebrity chefs, expert demonstrations and thousands of gift, home and food ideas to set you up for the festive period.

Whether you’re after a family day out, an inspiring shopping trip or simply the time to pamper yourself, this year’s seasonal show is bound to leave you feeling festive.

To claim one of 15,000 pairs of free tickets to this fantastic festive event in either London or Manchester follow the steps below:

1. Visit idealhomeshowchristmas.co.uk

2. Select the show you would like to visit either London or Manchester

3. Click the ‘book tickets’ link at the top right of the page

4. Enter the code SUN16 in the box next to ‘if you have a code please enter it here’ and click ‘USE CODE’

5. Select the date you would like to attend

6. Confirm the number of tickets

7. Enter contact details and click ‘book now’

8. A confirmation screen will show and you will receive an e-ticket to your selected email address within 3-5 days

Tickets will be valid for any day of the show, but you will be asked to select the day of your visit when booking.

Terms and conditions

Offer closes at 23.59pm on Wednesday October 26, 2016. A maximum of two free adult tickets (weekdays and limited weekend) per household. Children 15 years and under go for FREE.

Maximum one child per adult ticket. Please request these tickets at the time of booking.

Alternatively Children’s tickets will be available at the Box Office. Entry valid for the show and day requested between November 10-13, 2016 for EventCity Manchester and November 23-27, 2016 for Olympia London.

Tickets will be on a first-come, first-serve basis and will be print at home tickets only.

Tickets will be sent via email 3-5 days after application. Tickets are non-transferable, non-assignable and there are no cash alternatives. All tickets will be scanned upon entry. Multiple applications will be refused entry on site.

Open to UK residents only. Tickets include entry only.
