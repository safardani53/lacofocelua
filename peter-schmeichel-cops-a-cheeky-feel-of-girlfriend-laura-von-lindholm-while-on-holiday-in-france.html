Peter Schmeichel cops a cheeky feel of girlfriend Laura von Lindholm while on holiday in St Tropez

September 21, 2024

PETER SCHMEICHEL appears to be enjoying his summer holiday, as he cops a cheeky feel of girlfriend Laura von Lindholm while in St Tropez, France.

The former Manchester United goalkeeper, 53, donned a pair of bright yellow shorts as he accompanied the Danish actress at Club 55 in the south of France.

Schmeichel was enjoying a drink at the fancy bar-restaurant on the seafront with Von Lindholm, wearing a pair of smart black sunglasses.

The actress, wearing a black bikini and floppy hat was caught off guard by the cheeky Dane, who appeared to indulge in a squeeze of her backside at the club.

The pair were then spotted sharing a quick kiss while wading knee-deep in the Mediterranean.

Last season, Schmeichel was spotted at Leicester City's training ground as he took part in an interview ahead of the club's Champions League game with Atletico Madrid.

The Danish stopper spoke of his son Kasper - the No1 at the King Power Stadium - when Jamie Vardy hilariously interrupted the chat.

The England forward quipped: “Is it bring your father to work day? Is that what it is?”

An amused Schmeichel Snr replied “it is” before continuing with his interview.

Schmeichel spent eight seasons at Manchester United, winning five Premier League crowns to go with three FA Cups and the famous 1999 Champions League - part of the astonishing treble.
