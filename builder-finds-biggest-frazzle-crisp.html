Stunned builder finds worlds biggest Frazzle a whopping 4in long The Sun

August 18, 2024
blog

A BUILDER was left dazzled by a 4½in Frazzle — which could be a world record.

Harry Langley, 24, pulled out the bumper bacon snack, three times normal size, while eating lunch in his work van.

Harry told The Sun: “I didn’t actually notice it straightaway, which was weird because it was almost as big as the packet.

“The top end was sticking out, and as I pulled it out it just carried on going and going. I thought to myself ‘that’s massive’.”

Harry, who’d bought a multipack at B&M in Burton Latimer, Northants last week, added: “I was just fascinated. I took a pic and put it on Instagram. A few friends commented on its size.

“I genuinely think it is a Guinness World Record but don’t even know how you register that kind of thing.

“If they’re going to keep making them that size I’ll keep buying. I definitely got my money’s worth with that.”

Last month Sean Stewart, of North Lanarkshire, Scotland, posted a pic of a 4in Frazzle bought from Iceland.
