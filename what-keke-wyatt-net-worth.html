R&B SINGER and songwriter Keke Wyatt got her start in the music industry as a teenager.

What is Keke Wyatt's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Keke Wyatt has an estimated net worth of around $4million.

Keke has released five studio albums: Soul Sista, Who Knew? Unbelievable, Rated Love, and Keke Covers.

She also has one EP entitled Ke'Ke'.

In 2017, Keke was cast as a member of R&B Divas: Atlanta, where she starred in all three seasons of the show.

She also appeared in season six of Marriage Boot Camp: Reality Stars, as well as joining the 2014 Faith Evans music video for I Deserve It.

In 2009, Keke played the lead role of Je'Caryous Johnson in Love Overboard, a theatre production.

Is Keke Wyatt married?

Keke Wyatt has been married three times.

At the age of 18, Keke married her former road manager. Together, they share three children: Keyver Wyatt, Rahjah Ke', and Ke'Tarah Victoria.

After finalizing their divorce in 2009, Keke remarried the following year.

She and her second husband, Michael Jamar Ford, went on to have four children of their own: Ke'Mar Von, Wyatt Michael, Ke'Yoshi Bella, and Kendall Miguel.

Keke also helped to raise one of Ford's children from a prior relationship.

The couple officially divorced in the summer of 2018, and Keke remarried that October.

Her third and current husband, Zackariah Darring, is Keke's childhood friend and ex-boyfriend.

Together, they have two children and are expecting a third.
