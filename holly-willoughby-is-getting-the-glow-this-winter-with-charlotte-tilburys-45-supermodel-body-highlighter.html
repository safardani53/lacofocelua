SHE’S famed for her flawless golden skin.

And, Holly Willoughby, 37, has been getting her tan on this winter with the help of Charlotte Tilbury’s Supermodel Body highlighter.

The Dancing On Ice star’s make-up artist Patsy O’Neill revealed on Instagram that she uses the cult £45 product to get her glowing.

Described as “summer confidence in a bottle”, the highlighter gives “long-limbed, glossy-skinned perfection in minutes”.

It contains caffeine that detoxes the skin, aloe vera and allantoin to soothe, and menthol to stimulate blood circulation.

Charlotte Tilbury, who is Kate Moss' make-up guru, states: “The precious-metal highlighter mix catches the light down the centre of your limbs for a luminous radiant glow.

“Ideal if you want to make you skin feel firmer, suppler and smooth whilst getting that supermodel body.”

But this isn’t the first high-street beauty product Holly loves.

In January, we revealed she uses the Serum Foundation by The Ordinary, which costs just £6.

The Cult Beauty website says: “This unique formulation allows the pigments to look natural on the skin, stay on for longer and resist collecting within lines.

“In 21 versatile hues (with clever undertone identifiers) snap up your shade for seamless coverage – not to mention endless beauty brownie points.”

Holly has previously been a fan of BB Creams, particularly as they have a lack of cakiness.

She said to Marie Claire: “I’m not very good at wearing things on my skin, even foundation, I just don’t like the feel of it.

“So my BB cream is my best bet, it literally just feels like I’m wearing a moisturiser.”
