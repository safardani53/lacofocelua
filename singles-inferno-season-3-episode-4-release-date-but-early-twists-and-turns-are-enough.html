Single’s Inferno season 3 episode 4 is on its way, but Netflix viewers may be confused over why there are no more episodes available after 1, 2 and 3. A whole new line-up of singles are gathering on an island and have to form a connection to make their way into paradise, and escape the inferno. Singles Inferno has finally released its third season following the huge popularity of seasons 1 and 2. But Netflix fans won’t get all of the season in one go.

Single’s Inferno season 3 episode 4

Single’s Inferno season 3 episode 4 will be out on Tuesday, December 19, just under a week before Christmas Day. So, you have plenty of time to veg out on the sofa and catch up over the festive break!

Episodes guide for Single’s Inferno

Single’s Inferno season 3 episodes will hit Netflix each Tuesday. Episode dates past episode 5 are not confirmed by the streaming giant but are believed to follow a similar schedule to last year.

Episodes 4 and 5 air on Tuesday, December 19. Contestants have to make strong connections without sharing persona information, but after a spark, the rule changes.
