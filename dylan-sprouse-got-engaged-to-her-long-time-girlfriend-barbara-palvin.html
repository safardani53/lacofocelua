Dylan Sprouse Got Engaged To Her Long-Time Girlfriend Barbara Palvin

June 28, 2024
blog

Dylan Sprouse, an American performer, is allegedly betrothed to model Barbara Palvin, his lifelong partner. According to Entertainment Tonight, the 30-year-old Suite Life of Zack and Cody actor recently proposed to the 29-year-old model, and the couple is very happy together. According to the person who broke the news:

“Dylan and Barbara have gotten engaged.” They have been showing off her band to everyone and are overjoyed. They’re always in love with and by each other’s sides, but they’re even happy than normal. They are ecstatic to begin this new journey together.”

Dylan Sprouse and Barbara earlier ignited engagement speculation in early March when they were photographed together at the Mammoth Film Festival with a band on her finger. However, representatives for the personalities did not corroborate the story at the moment.

Dylan Sprouse and Barbara Palvin met At a gathering.

Dylan Sprouse met Barbara Palvin for the first time at a gathering in 2017. He allegedly left his phone number in her DMs after the gathering. Sprouse explained the situation to W Magazine in 2019:

“She followed me, so I figured I’d give her something.” And I slipped into her direct messages. ‘Hey, I’m not sure how long you’ll be in New York, but we should chill out if you want,’ I said. ‘Here’s my phone number.’ And she didn’t contact me in six months.”

Barbara Palvin traveled to China for their first date to meet the protagonist of The Suite Life of Zack and Cody. Barbara eventually texted Sprouse while he was in China for a six-month production. According to People Magazine, she stated:

“After talking for three months, I flew to China to see him.”

Palvin claimed that she was slated to work in China but that the assignment had been postponed. She allegedly questioned Sprouse if it was strange that she still wanted to travel to China and see him, to which he replied, “Please come.” Barbara disclosed that Dylan had requested exclusivity, but she had declined because she wanted to get to know him better. She did, however, ultimately agree:

“And then I said, ‘OK, let’s do it.’… I just sat down, took a big breath, and thought to myself, “What am I waiting for?” There is no other man I would adore more than him, and he has everything I need.”

The couple started dating in June 2018 and made their romance Instagram public on Dylan’s birthday that year. Barbara captioned a photo of herself with Dylan Sprouse the “biggest present of all.” Since then, the pair has shared several red carpet-appearances. Palvin described Sprouse as the “perfect guy” in an interview with Vogue Australia.

“Having a partner is unusual for me. I had been unmarried for six years. It paid off; I believe I have discovered the ideal man. He’s very tender and kind. That’s what I do on my days off: I spend them with the individuals I care about, and I’m in love right now.”

The couple moved in together in Brooklyn, New York, in January 2019. Dylan Sprouse and Barbara have since attended several star-studded events, including the Oscars afterparty and Victoria’s Secret performance. They are now a popular favored pair.
