Where the Kardashians' forgotten exes are now featuring Reggie Bush, Lamar Odom &amp; Tyga

May 02, 2024
blog

OH sure, everyone knows the ongoing drama between Kim Kardashian and estranged husband Kanye West.

Or that Kourtney Kardashian is getting ready to walk down the aisle with Travis Barker.

Or even that any day now, Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott will welcome their second child together.

But what about the Kardashian rejects?

For every Travis Barker, there is a Joe Francis that has been dropped by the famous family.

Take a look back at some of these forgotten Kardashian relationships, and find out whatever became of the broken hearted.

KIM KARDASHIAN

NICK CANNON

Nick and Kim dated from 2006 to 2007, reportedly splitting over her infamous sex tape.

Nick was married to Mariah Carey whom he also has children with.

Nick, 41, is now the father of seven children with four different women. Three of his children were born in 2021.

He has found fame hosting the hit reality show The Masked Singer.

REGGIE BUSH

Kim began dating NFL running back Reggie Bush in 2007, but their relationship fizzled out after two years.

He then dated country singer Jesse James in 2010 before he met dancer and choreographer Lilit Avagyan

The couple dated for two years before making their way down the aisle after the birth of their first child.

The 36-year-old currently an analyst for Fox Sports, and is promoting the new spring professional football league The USFL.

KRIS HUMPHRIES

Kim and basketballer Kris got engaged in the Summer of 2011, only seven months after they began dating.

However, their happiness was short-lived as Kim filed for divorce on October 31, just shy of three months after they said "I do."

Kris retired from the NBA on March 26, 2019 and now lives a quiet life out of the spotlight.

The 36-year-old opened a chain of Five Guys restaurants in Minnesota and Wisconsin, and became a real estate agent.

Kris was spotted earlier this year getting cozy with model Neyleen Ashley.

KHLOE KARDASHIAN

LAMAR ODOM

"My name is Lamar Odom," the former basketball player recently announced on Instagram. "People may know me for winning two championship with the L.A. Lakers.

"And they also may know me for me marrying Khloe Kardashian. We had our own TV show,"

Lamar, 42, was referring to the couple's spin-off series on E! fittingly titled: Khloe and Lamar.

Khloe and Lamar were married from 2009-2016.

He's currently preparing to appear on the third season of Celebrity Big Brother, along with Travis Barker's ex Shanna Moakler.

FRENCH MONTANA

French, 37, had a highly publicized romance with Khloe, back in 2014 after she had split from her husband Lamar Odom.

After about eight months of dating, the couple broke up that September.

The pair have since have remained friends and have been spotted at various events together, including at Kanye West's church service.

JAMES HARDEN

NBA All-Star James Harden dated Khloe Kardashian for eight months between 2015-2016.

Following their breakup, Harden, 32, opened up to Sports Illustrated in 2017 saying that he "didn't like all the attention" that came with dating a member of the Kardashian clan.

Now playing for the Brooklyn Nets, Harden is considered one of basketball's most prolific scorers and has been called the best shooting guard in the NBA.

KOURTNEY KARDASHIAN

JOE FRANCIS

Girls Gone Wild founder Joe Francis has been close with the Kardashian family for decades, and even briefly dated Kourtney, before she skyrocketed to fame. 

More than 15 years after their breakup, Joe, 48, had high praise about his former girlfriend but slammed the man his ex would go on to have three children with. 

During the latest episode of the Behind The Velvet Rope podcast with David Yontef, the TV entrepreneur dissed Scott Disick, explaining: “Scott… he didn't have two nickels to rub to his name and they made him wealthy. 

“And Scott doesn't have a family. Both his parents died. You know, I feel bad for that. But Scott's a total dork."

Scott was arrested in 2020 for a domestic violence incident at his home.

JUSTIN BIEBER

It's not totally clear what kind of relationship these two had back in 2015, but they weren't shy when it came to setting the rumor mill straight.

After reports emerged that the two had been hooking up Bieber posted a snap with a mystery woman, believed to be Kourtney, with the caption "Lord knows" - an apparent reference to her baby daddy Scott "The Lord" Disick.

Justin has been married to Hallie Baldwin since 2019.

KENDALL JENNER

A$AP ROCKY

Long before heading under Rhianna's umbrella, rapper A$AP Rocky dated Kendall Jenner.

The two confirmed their relationship after attending the Met Gala on May 1, 2017.

At the time of their relationship, the couple were photographed clubbing together in various locations worldwide and even attended the Paris and Milan Fashion Week in 2017.

A$AP and Kendall dated from 2016 to 2017, however, split due to difficult schedules.

HARRY STYLES

Kendall and Harry were first spotted getting cozy together in 2013 after they were introduced by their managers.

They split and subsequently sparked reconciliation rumors two years later when they were seen on a yacht in Anguilla.

They reunited at the 2019 Met Gala and again months later on The Late Late Show With James Corden.

Although Kendall and Harry have never openly spoken about their relationship, Harry was said to have written a song about his alleged former fling.

The former One Direction singer is currently on a solo tour.

ORLANDO BLOOM

The Pirates of the Caribbean star was rumored to have gone on a number of secret dates with the runway model throughout the summer of 2015.

The 45-year-old is nearly twice as old as 26-year-old Kendall, but that didn't deter the short-term couple.

A source at the time revealed the pair have been seeing each other secretly, saying: "The age difference doesn't seem to bother them although it's far too early for either to tell if it's going to develop into something serious."

Orlando is currently engaged to singer Katy Perry

KYLIE JENNER

DRAKE

The KUWTK star, 24, and rapper, 35, have been friends for years, with Drake even performing at Kylie's 16th birthday party in 2013.

Kylie has been linked to Drake numerous times over the years.

They first sparked dating rumors in 2019 after US Weekly claimed their relationship was more than platonic with a source revealing that their friendship "did take a romantic turn."

There was never any official announcement from the pair and Kylie went on to rekindle her relationship with Travis Scott while Drake was linked to model Imaan Hamman in February 2021.

As of June 2021, he has been linked to model Johanna Leia after the two were spotted at the Sierra Canyon basketball game.

TYGA

Beauty mogul Kylie Jenner publicly dated rapper Tyga on and off starting in 2014.

The pair met in November of 2011 at Kendall Jenner's 16th birthday party when Kylie was 14 years old and Tyga was 21.

They went public on social media in 2014 as Jenner posted photos of them celebrating her 17th birthday together.

In September of 2016, Kylie showed off a massive ring on Instagram leaving her fans wondering if she and Tyga were engaged or not.
