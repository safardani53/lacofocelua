Tracey Gould sentenced to probation following DUI / Jossip



Tue / 22 Mar 2005 Tracey Gould sentenced to probation following DUI » More Celebs

You might think Tracey Gould grew out of her awkward phase on the appropriately titled Growing Pains, but such is not the case. At yesterday’s sentencing for Tracey’s 2004 DUI, the former Carol Seaver received three years’ probation, 30 days of county work release, 240 hours of community service and had her license suspended for a year.

Only because she’s used her “celebrity” to alert others of the dangers of drunk driving (who knew?!) did the judge reduce Gould’s community service hours. At least she’ll have something else to talk about beside her battle with anorexia on her her public service announcements.
