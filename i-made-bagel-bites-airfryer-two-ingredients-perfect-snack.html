I made bagel bites in my Air Fryer and they make the perfect snack

March 25, 2024
blog

YOU can make just about anything in an Air Fryer these days. 

They have become one of the most convenient appliances to have in your kitchen. 

They are also fantastic for making a quick easy snack on the go. 

On Tiktok account thisisplanetfood has shared a viral video of a great air fryer snack recipe. 

Here’s how they made quick and easy bagel bites.

First you grab yourself a bagel and slice it.

Read more airfryer stories

Now you don’t want to halfway the way you usually would to make a sandwich. 

You want to slice the bagel around the middle so you get small little circles. 

Then you will want to put these in a bowl and sprinkle over some olive oil. 

If you wish, you can season the bagel bites with salt and pepper or even some peri sauce and peri salt like the creator did in their video.

Then you simply pop them into the air fryer and at 170 degrees for five minutes. 

Once done, your bagel bites will be crispy and delicious.

The creator in this video had their bagel bites with a Nandos Perinaise sauce. 

But you could have this snack with any sauce you wanted to or have them on their own. 

And you don’t have to use a plain bagel for this recipe.

You can try other flavours like sesame or red onion and chive bagels. 

This is an adaptable recipe as you could turn this from savoury to sweet but using Cinnamon and raisin bagels.

These would make a great snack to pack in lunches or eat on the go.

The video has now gone viral with over 4.7 million views and views are raving about the recipe. 

One commenter said: “I used an everything bagel when I did this, so delish!”

Another commenter said: “I'm making these every day one day would make for a great lunch snack.”
