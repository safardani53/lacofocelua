Ashley Graham poses nude with Rita Ora on Americas Next Top Model

ASHLEY Graham poses totally nude on an upcoming episode of America’s Next Top Model.

Alongside Rita Ora, plus size model Ashley whips her clothes off in front of the cameras to encourage the wannabe models to do the same.

In a clip for the up-coming episode, the top 13 models are asked to pose for their first totally nude shoot.

Calling them into a room, Ashley shows them how it’s done, posing artistically with hundreds of pearls.

She tells the waiting models: “Take it all off and come on in!”

Later she flashes her bare breasts as she gives them a motivational speech.

Ashley says: “You saw that I just did this, I got curves, I’ve got cellulite, I’ve got bits about myself that I don’t like or that have people have told me I shouldn't like about myself.

“Any of you that feel self-conscious right now remember I just took all of my clothes off, so own your confidence!”

Rita waits for the women to come through one by one with Ashley but one of the models isn’t enjoying the experience.

Speaking to the cameras, India says: “I just can’t whip off a robe.”

Their assignment comes just hours after all the models were given a makeover.

Some were furious with the results and tears flowed throughout the episode.

Others embraced the change and loved their new looks.

Rita joined the show earlier this year, taking over from former supermodel Tyra Banks.

Speaking about her role, Rita – who’s set to release her second album in the New Year – said: “I never wanted to fill Tyra's shoes. I just wanted to create new ones.

“I sat down with Tyra and her team, and we just sort of said, if I was to do this show, how are we going to make it brand new so that it doesn't look like ‘Rita against Tyra,’ you know what I'm saying? That's when the rebranding happened.”

Talking to Pop Sugar, Rita added: “Instead of us finding a beautiful girl who takes a beautiful photo, it's about finding a beautiful girl who can take a great photo and become a brand, have a following, and leave the competition being a threat to the Gigis and the Bellas.

“That, for me, is what we are trying to find.”
