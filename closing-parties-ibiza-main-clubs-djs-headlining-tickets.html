With the summer season now extended to mid October, there's still plenty of time to get some last-minute clubbing in. These are the closing party dates for the island's biggest clubs...

What are the main clubs in Ibiza?

Clubs like Pacha, Amnesia and DC-10 have such a cult following that their reputations precede them here in Britain.

Some of the clubs you might remember from your youth, like Space, have closed down - but new party destinations soon pop up in their place.

Space was reopened by the team behind upmarket hotel Ushuaia in 2015, and is now called Hi Ibiza.

While gone are the days that people came back from Ibiza whiter than they went away.

Day party venues like Ocean Beach, the Zoo Project, Bora Bora Beach, Blue Marlin and Ushuaia are also attracting thousands of people - and big name DJs - every summer.

Cafe Mambo also has a cult following on the sunset strip, and offers pre-club DJ shows.

Or visit one of the club atmosphere restaurants like STK in Ibiza Town.

For a cheaper alternative, head to Cafe Mambo's sister restaurant Savannah -  is just two doors down.

How can I get tickets for the Ibiza closing parties?

For a one-stop shop for Ibiza club tickets, visit Spotlight.

You can also buy direct through each club's website, or from promoters out in Ibiza - although this is often more expensive.

If it's not a sell-out event, tickets should also be available on the door.
