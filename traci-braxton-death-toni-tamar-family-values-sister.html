Who was Traci Braxton?

Traci Braxton was a singer, reality star, and radio personality.

Crash & Burn, Braxton's solo debut album, was released in October 2014, following the success of the single Last Call.

Last Call reached number 16 on the R&B Adult chart in the US.

Crash & Burn debuted at No. 108 on the Billboard Hot 200 in its first week, selling 4,000 copies, and went on to No. 11 on the relaunched Billboard R&B Albums list and No. 1 on the Heatseekers Albums chart.

She released the track Broken Things in April 2018 with her sisters Toni, Towanda, and Trina.

Braxton's second album, On Earth, was released later that month, and the song Lifeline was released as the official lead single in August 2018.
