DIPLO has a long dating history that includes multiple women in and out of the spotlight.

Apart from DJing and producing, the 44-year-old is also a father of three.

Who is Diplo?

Diplo - whose real name is Thomas Wesley Pentz - is a house DJ, songwriter and record producer.

His stage name comes from diplodocus from his childhood fascination with dinosaurs.

The Mississippi native is the co-creator and lead member of the electronic dancehall music project Major Lazer and a member of the supergroup LSD, with Sia and Labrinth.

He is also working with producer and DJ Skrillex as part of the electronic duo Jack Ü, as well as on Silk City with Mark Ronson.

Diplo is the founder of the record company, Mad Decent, as well as the nonprofit organization, Heaps Decent.

In his long career, he has worked with artists like Gwen Stefani, Britney Spears, Madonna, Justin Bieber, Usher, Snoop Dogg, Chris Brown, and more.

Diplo's net worth is estimated to be $50million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

How many baby mamas does Diplo have?

Diplo has two baby mamas and three children.

Kathryn Lockhart

He welcomed his two sons - Lockett and Lazer - with ex, Kathryn Lockhart.

She is an actress known for starring in the films Unwelcome (2017) and Gravid (2018), according to her IMDb.

Lockhart has over 15k followers on Instagram, where she often posts pictures of her children - including her newest edition, baby, Lennox, whom she shares with another partner.

Jevon King

Diplo shares his son, Pace, with Jevon King.

King is a Trinidadian model and beauty pageant titleholder who won Miss Universe Trinidad and Tobago 2014.

In an hour-long Instagram interview with motherhood blogger, Alexan Stewart, King claimed that Pace was conceived through in vitro fertilization or IVF.

What has Diplo said about his sexuality?

While making an appearance on Emily Ratajkowski's March 14, 2023, High Low podcast episode, Diplo admitted he had received oral sex from a man sometime in the past.

He told Emily that he isn’t “aroused by men that much,” though he noted: “I’m sure I’ve gotten a blow job from a guy before. … For sure. 100 [percent], yeah.”
